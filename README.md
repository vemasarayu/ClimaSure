##  Overview
Gig economy delivery workers are essential to modern urban logistics. However, their income is highly vulnerable to sudden weather disruptions such as heavy rainfall, extreme heat, floods, and pollution.

ClimaSure is an AI-powered parametric insurance platform that provides **automatic compensation** when weather conditions prevent workers from completing deliveries.

It eliminates manual claims using:

* Real-time weather data
* Severity-based triggers
* AI-driven validation

---

##  Problem Statement

Delivery workers rely on daily earnings. Weather disruptions can instantly stop work.

### Key Challenges:

* Loss of income during bad weather
* No structured income protection
* Manual claims are slow and inefficient
* High risk of fraudulent claims

---

##  Target Persona

**Food Delivery Worker**

* Platforms: Swiggy / Zomato
* Daily Earnings: ₹600–₹800
* Deliveries: 15–20/day

 Problem: Earnings drop to zero during severe weather

 Solution: ClimaSure provides **weekly coverage + automatic payouts**

---

##  Platform Workflow

1. Worker registers
2. System tracks location & weather
3. Weekly premium calculated
4. Weather severity monitored
5. Activity validated
6. Claim triggered automatically
7. Payout calculated
8. Amount credited instantly

---

##  Key Features

###  Parametric Weather Detection

Real-time data from rainfall, temperature, and AQI APIs

###  Severity-Based Payout Model

| Severity | Condition                  | Impact       | Payout      |
| -------- | -------------------------- | ------------ | ----------- |
| Low      | Light rain / moderate heat | Partial work | ₹100–₹200   |
| Medium   | Heavy rain / high AQI      | Reduced work | ₹300–₹500   |
| High     | Flood / extreme heat       | No work      | Full payout |

---

###  Delivery Activity Verification

###  Baseline Income Modeling
